History

RaftingTours360 is a trip agency focused on providing the best experience in rafting tours.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by Alex Carter and Mia Rivera, RaftingTours360 began as a small operation near the Colorado River, offering guided tours with a commitment to safety and eco-friendly practices.

Today, we offer tours across North America for all skill levels, from beginner-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids.
